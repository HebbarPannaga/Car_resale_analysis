# Used Car Sales Analysis

## Project Overview

This project performs exploratory and business-oriented analysis on used car sales data using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn. The analysis focuses on identifying key factors influencing vehicle resale value, revenue trends, and depreciation patterns.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze revenue contribution by vehicle brands and models.
- Understand the impact of vehicle age, mileage, and condition on selling price.
- Identify brands with better resale value through depreciation analysis.
- Explore pricing patterns and correlations among key variables.
- Generate business insights to support inventory and pricing decisions.

---

## Dataset Features

- Vehicle Details: Year, Make, Model, Trim, Body Type, Transmission
- Vehicle Condition: Condition, Odometer Reading
- Pricing Information: MMR, Selling Price
- Location Information: State
- Sales Information: Sale Date, Seller

## Key Insights

- Revenue is concentrated among a few manufacturers and models.
- Vehicle age and mileage have a strong negative impact on resale value.
- Better-condition vehicles command significant price premiums.
- Some brands retain their value better than others, resulting in lower depreciation.
- MMR shows a strong positive relationship with selling price.
- Vehicles exceeding 100k miles experience noticeable price deterioration.
